    Secure federal contracts for Innovations from the Hart LLC (CAGE:17CR1, UEI:V6B2FVW9QCK9) leveraging core agent capabilities.
    
    ## Phases:
    
    1.  **Foundation & Registration (Months 1-2):**
        * Verify SAM.gov registration is active and complete for CAGE:17CR1, UEI:V6B2FVW9QCK9.
        * Identify relevant NAICS codes based on core agent capabilities (Chancellor: Strategic Planning, Forge Master: Prototyping/R&D, Revenue Architect: Financial Modeling/Optimization, Artisan: Specialized Craftsmanship/Design).

    2.  **Capability Statement & Market Research (Months 2-3):**
        * Develop a compelling capability statement highlighting Innovations from the Hart LLC's strengths, CAGE, UEI, and core agent expertise.

    3.  **Networking & Teaming (Months 3-6):**

        * Identify potential prime contractors for subcontracting opportunities and teaming agreements.
        * Build relationships with Small Business Liaisons (SBLs) and contracting officers at target agencies.
    
    4.  **Proposal Development (Ongoing):**
        * Develop a streamlined proposal development process.
        * Tailor proposals to specific solicitation requirements, clearly referencing CAGE/UEI and how our core agents address the need.
        * Focus on small business set-asides initially to build past performance.
    
    5.  **Performance & Growth (Ongoing):**
        * Deliver exceptional performance on initial contracts.
        * Gather positive CPARS ratings.
        * Leverage past performance to pursue larger and more complex contracts.
